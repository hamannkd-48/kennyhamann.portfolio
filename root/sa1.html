Comprehensive Case Study

Sonoma Academy

A multi-faceted design engagement encompassing institutional branding, student publication identity, and academic design explorations.

25th Anniversary Visual Identity

Commissioned in late 2025 to commemorate a quarter-century of excellence. The goal was to create a logo system that honors the school's heritage while maintaining a modern, progressive aesthetic for the next chapter of Sonoma Academy.

SA Paw Print Digital Rebranding

Collaborated with the student-run newspaper to modernize their digital presence. The project focused on a high-impact social media identity, starting with a bold profile image and extending into post templates and storytelling frameworks.

Graphic Design & Product Concepts

A series of works exploring design history, typography, and product packaging. These projects blend theoretical research with practical application.

Design Movement Poster

A typographic exploration studying the principles of the Bauhaus and Swiss Design movements, emphasizing grid systems and objective clarity.

Wild Peak Yerba Mate

Final project for Graphic Design elective: A comprehensive packaging concept for a theoretical beverage company, featuring custom illustration and bold color blocking.

Institutional Collateral

Extended work for the Sonoma Academy community, ranging from UI/UX concepts for the institutional homepage to promotional event materials.

Homepage UI Redesign

A conceptual UI/UX update for the school's digital gateway, focusing on accessibility, modern aesthetics, and intuitive navigation paths.

Event Promotion

Visual collateral for the 'Shade of Jade' concert series, combining vibrant color palettes with rhythmic typographic arrangements.
