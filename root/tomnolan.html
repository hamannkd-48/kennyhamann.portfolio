    Tom Nolan Band

    Brand Redesign

    In the spring of 2024, I was contracted by the Tom Nolan Band to redesign their brand identity. I surveyed their current state, met with band members, and created a new and improved look.

    The new look included new colors and fonts built on the surveyed tenets and values of the brand. It was wonderful to work with Tom. This project has concluded. To the left is the compiled brandboard summarizing my work for Tom.
